Best Korean Dramas According to Cherrypie :)

My Top 5 Dramas of All Time :)

Goblin

This Drama is GOAT. The Goblin lives for almost a thousand years. One day he finds a bride who can take the sword that had been stuck in his body for thousand years, and lift the curse of immortality. But he falls in love with her. So, they fight their way for their love.

Ture Beauty

This is a high school Drama. Where a girl named Lim-ju-kyung has a hard time making friends because she has bumps all around her face. And one day she had to transfer to a new school and she even changes her looks. She learns how to put makeup and turns out she is the most prettiest girl in her whole school. Later she falls in love with a guy named Lee-Soo-Ho who knows her real face so does he. But Soo-Ho's friend Han-Seo-Jun falls in love with Lim-Ju-Kyung. He is known as the most loved second lead of all time.

Home Town Cha-Cha-Cha

This story runs around a boy and a girl who have two completely different perspectives of life. They used to hate eachother. But eventually falls in love. And the story goes on.

A Love So Beautiful

This story makes you feel good. Despite her bad looks the boy falls for a girl who pursues him for a long time.They finally make their way to a happy ending.

Hwarang

This is a fictional Drama based on Korean History. When the King was murdered the Qeen sent her son to a safety. When her son has come of age, she prepares a warrior group named "Hwarang" and makes them servants of the King (her son). But they fight their way to serve the truth.
